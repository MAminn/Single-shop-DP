## Template Selection Guide

### Choose **CategoryGridClassic** if:

- You want a versatile, professional default
- Your category has many products
- You need strong filtering capabilities
- General e-commerce use case

### Choose **CategoryHeroSplit** if:

- You have beautiful hero imagery
- Lifestyle/fashion products
- Visual storytelling is important
- Modern, trendy aesthetic wanted

### Choose **CategoryMinimal** if:

- Premium/luxury brand
- Professional services
- Typography matters
- Clean, distraction-free browsing
- Fast loading is critical

### Choose **CategoryShowcase** if:

- Flagship/premium collection
- You want to highlight featured products
- Strong brand presence needed
- Editorial-style presentation
- High-impact visual storytelling

---

## How to Switch Templates

1. Navigate to: `/dashboard/admin/templates`
2. Select "Category Page" from dropdown
3. Choose your preferred template
4. Click to set as active
5. Visit any category page to see the change

---

## Content Editing

1. Navigate to: `/dashboard/admin/category-content`
2. Select a category from dropdown
3. Edit:
   - Title
   - Description (toggle + text)
   - Hero image (toggle + URL)
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. Preview the category page

---

## Notes

- All templates are **production-ready**
- All templates are **fully responsive**
- All templates meet **ThemeForest quality standards**
- Layout is **fixed** (merchants control content only)
- Templates can be **mixed** (different categories can use different templates)
